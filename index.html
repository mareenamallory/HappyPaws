FAQs

Holistic means entirely of a whole, used by pet foods as 'natural' and wholesome, with all ingredients being digestible & nutritious!

For Happy Paws, this means:
• Brown rice instead of brewers (white) rice which is just a filler. Brown rice is a complex carb which is digestible and not just a starch.
• No ground corn. Ground corn is not very digestible. Corn gluten meal, which is the protein finnings of corn, is digestible but some cats and dogs are allergic to the proteins or carbs contained in corn.
• No white potatoes.
• No wheat grains.
• No meat meal or bone meal.

Happy Paws is Fresh & Natural. It is made and delivered directly to our distributors and naturally preserved with vitamine E and vitamine C. There are no artificial colours, flavours, or chemical additives!

At Happy Paws we only use Top Grade chicken, BC fish meal (fresh ocean fish), and 100% New Zealand free range Lamb-meat which is cooked into meal. No by-products! Fruits, veggies, and sea salt are then added!

In our pursuit of excellence, we combine all these natural and nutritious ingredients for a total holistic-healthy food for the complete wellness of our pets.

Our dry food maintains its nutritional value and taste for up to 18 months unopened. After opening, it should be kept cool, dry, and eaten within 6 months for the best freshness!

Happy Paws uses vitamin E and vitamin C, nature's antioxidant. Vitamin E occurs naturally in plant oils to protect against oxidation and to maintain the original flavour, aroma, and colour of our fresh meats. Vitamin C occurs in the natural form of citric acid.
